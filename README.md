# Solar Assist Electric Vehicle
> 🚀 Solar-assisted EV project focused on improving energy efficiency using photovoltaic integration and load optimization.

This project focuses on utilizing solar energy to enhance the efficiency of electric vehicles by integrating photovoltaic systems with battery-powered EV technology.

## Abstract
This project addresses the growing energy crisis by exploring renewable energy sources such as solar power. It demonstrates how photovoltaic principles can be used to generate electricity and assist electric vehicles, reducing dependence on fossil fuels.

## Introduction
Electric Vehicles (EVs) are gaining popularity due to their low emissions and environmental benefits. This project integrates solar energy into EV systems to improve efficiency and sustainability.

## Working Principle
- Solar panels convert sunlight into electrical energy using photovoltaic cells
- Energy is stored in rechargeable batteries
- The motor uses stored energy to drive the vehicle
- Additional energy is recovered during braking

## System Components
- Solar Panels
- Battery Storage System
- Motor and Motor Controller
- Energy Storage System
- Drivetrain

## Advantages
- Low operating cost
- Zero vehicular emissions
- Renewable energy utilization
- Minimal maintenance requirements

## Limitations
- Limited energy storage capacity
- High initial cost
- Performance depends on sunlight availability
- Larger surface area required for solar panels

## Conclusion
Solar-assisted electric vehicles provide an eco-friendly alternative to conventional vehicles and contribute to reducing environmental pollution and fossil fuel dependency.

## Future Scope
Solar EV technology has strong future potential. With advancements in solar efficiency and battery technology, it can become a practical and sustainable transportation solution.
